Documentation of our progress with Pepper

This site was developed for a Study Project at the Institute of Cognitive Science, University of Osnabrück. We have been working with Pepper robot since 2017. This year, our goal is to achieve a complex exercise (such as the Robocup Cocktail Party) by integrating the work of different groups.

The symbols below represent functionality, coding, and teamwork. Keep scrolling to see our groups. We hope you find this documentation of our work with Pepper helpful!
